Which of the following are not legal C identifiers?

(a) 100_bottles
(b) _100_bottles
(c) one__hundred__bottles
(d) bottles_by_the_hundred_

Answer:
(a) 100_bottles, because it starts with a digit. 
Other illegal identifiers include those that are C compilation keywords, 
or those that contain dashes (-), spaces, or special characters.

Dangerous identifiers include those starting with _X (where X is any capital letter), 
or those starting with a single underscore (_) or two underscores (__).
